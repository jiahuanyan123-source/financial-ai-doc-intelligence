# Issuer Credit Note

Issuer: North River Infrastructure Group
Rating: AAA
Industry: Transportation Infrastructure
Ownership: Provincial SOE

## Business Profile

North River Infrastructure Group operates toll roads, rail transit assets, and
urban infrastructure projects in a provincial capital region. The issuer has
strategic importance to the local government and receives recurring government
support for key projects.

The company has a large investment pipeline. Several rail transit and hub
projects require continued capital expenditure over the next three years.

## Financial Summary

Revenue: 42.5 bn
EBITDA: 8.2 bn
Total debt: 78.0 bn
Short-term debt: 18.5 bn
Cash: 12.0 bn
Operating cash flow: 5.1 bn
Capital expenditure: 9.8 bn
Interest expense: 2.4 bn

## Risk Notes

Debt growth accelerated after the start of the rail transit expansion plan.
Free cash flow remained negative because capital expenditure exceeded operating
cash flow.

The issuer uses perpetual debt instruments in part of its financing structure.
Several subsidiaries provide external guarantees for affiliated infrastructure
companies.

There is no known material litigation disclosed in this note.
