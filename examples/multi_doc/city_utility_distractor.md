# Harbor City Utility Services - Credit Note

Issuer: Harbor City Utility Services
Rating: AA
Industry: Water and Environmental Services
Ownership: City-Owned Enterprise

## Business Profile

Harbor City Utility Services operates water supply, wastewater treatment, and
solid waste assets. The company has recurring tariff revenue and receives
targeted government grants for environmental upgrades.

The utility has a smaller investment program than transport infrastructure
issuers. Capital expenditure focuses on treatment capacity and pipeline renewal.

## Financial Summary

Revenue: 18.6 bn
EBITDA: 4.1 bn
Total debt: 29.4 bn
Short-term debt: 6.8 bn
Cash: 3.2 bn
Operating cash flow: 3.7 bn
Capital expenditure: 4.5 bn
Interest expense: 0.9 bn

## Risk Notes

The company faces tariff-adjustment uncertainty and rising maintenance costs.
External guarantees are limited to one small environmental subsidiary.

Government grants support part of the wastewater upgrade program, but the note
does not assume extraordinary rescue support.
