# East Bay Urban Development - Credit Note

Issuer: East Bay Urban Development
Rating: AA+
Industry: Urban Development
Ownership: Municipal SOE

## Business Profile

East Bay Urban Development builds industrial parks, municipal roads, and public
housing projects. The issuer receives policy support, but its strategic role is
more local than provincial.

The company has a moderate project pipeline. New land development projects are
expected to slow after the current construction cycle.

## Financial Summary

Revenue: 31.2 bn
EBITDA: 5.6 bn
Total debt: 54.0 bn
Short-term debt: 9.0 bn
Cash: 15.5 bn
Operating cash flow: 4.4 bn
Capital expenditure: 5.2 bn
Interest expense: 1.8 bn

## Risk Notes

Debt growth remained stable because most park construction is near completion.
Free cash flow was slightly negative, but the deficit narrowed from the prior
year.

The issuer has no material external guarantee exposure disclosed in this note.
Liquidity pressure is manageable because cash exceeds short-term debt.
